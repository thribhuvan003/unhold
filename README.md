# Unhold

**An AI case manager for bank & UPI account freezes.**

Account frozen out of nowhere? Unhold helps you understand why it happened, get your documents in order, and move through resolution step by step — with AI guidance instead of guesswork.

## What it does

- **Explains the freeze** — turns a confusing notice into clear, plain-language next steps
- **Verifies documents** — an OCR checker validates uploads before you submit them
- **Guides resolution** — NVIDIA NIM models walk you through each case, step by step
- **Keeps cases moving** — scheduled background jobs track deadlines and follow-ups

## Tech stack

Next.js 16 · TypeScript · Supabase (Postgres + Auth) · NVIDIA NIM · OCR · Vercel
