## Core Short Answers

**Q: "Why not just call the bank or file on cybercrime.gov.in?"**

"Just calling or filing NCRP is the right first step. In practice, for many innocent people (especially in money trails), the bank waits for cyber cell clearance. 2026 SOP gives banks 7 days + IO 15 days, but real cases often drag for weeks/months with zero updates. A proper written package with verified docs + formal escalation to the right officer is what actually creates movement and a record. That's what Unhold helps organize."

**Q: "My friend got released by just calling."**

"Happens in simple cases. Many others report 1-2+ months of 'complaint closed but still frozen', no replies, multiple branch visits. When the trail is longer, casual calls get ignored. Written, timestamped submissions with proof move faster because they create accountability."

**Q: "Will this unfreeze my account faster?"**

"We don't control banks or police. We can't promise speed or success. What we can do is help you submit stronger, verified evidence (SHA-256 sealed bundle) + proper escalation letters with proof you sent them. Many people who succeed mention exactly that kind of organized follow-up."

**Q: "Why upload documents to an app? Privacy?"**

"Fair question. You control what you upload. Documents get SHA-256 verified so the version you download/send is provably the same. Nothing is auto-sent anywhere. You still decide what to give the bank/police. Alternative today is emailing loose files or carrying printouts anyway."

**Q: "This is just paperwork, I can do it myself."**

"True. Google + persistence works. The issue is most people whose salary is locked are stressed and disorganized. Unhold gives guided questions tailored to what SBI/cyber cell actually ask, verified uploads, a single sealed bundle instead of 10 screenshots, letter templates, and a timeline of what you did. It's a tool to do the work better and faster when you're already in a bad spot."

**Q: "Is this legal? Are you promising to solve it?"**

"We're not lawyers. We don't file with police or RBI. Everything is copy-only — you review and send yourself. Clear disclaimers in every letter: no guarantees. We help with organization and verification. The responsibility and the sending stays with you."

**Q: "How much does it cost? Why not free?"**

(If pricing exists in future — currently focus on value) "The core value is saving time, reducing mistakes, and producing a stronger package when your own money is at stake. Many people spend weeks figuring this out the hard way."

## Direct answer to "Claude/ChatGPT with a detailed prompt can write the letters and mails for me"

"Claude or GPT can write text if you paste your story and ask nicely. That's true.

Unhold is different because it does three things a chat cannot do well:

1. It verifies your actual documents (SHA-256 + AI analysis) and creates one sealed PDF bundle with cryptographic hashes — not loose screenshots attached to an email.
2. It enforces the real escalation process (you must upload proof you sent L1 before L2 becomes available). This creates a clean, credible record.
3. It maintains a persistent, timestamped audit trail of everything in one place that you can actually show the bank or Ombudsman.

A one-off LLM chat gives you words. Unhold helps you build the professional, verifiable package that banks respond to when generic complaints stall.

You can still use Claude to double-check the drafts if you want. The point of Unhold is the structure, verification, and proof tracking — not just generating text."

3. Offer value without defensiveness: "If you want help organizing the documents + letters properly, the tool is there. No pressure."
4. Link or stop.

Never argue "our thing is better." Stick to facts about the process.
