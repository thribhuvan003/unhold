## Slightly Longer Version (if they want more details)

**Why this exists**

A lot of normal people (students, salary guys, small shop owners) get their account frozen because money came from someone who was later linked to fraud. Even if you did nothing wrong.

Banks freeze first and ask questions later. They often want:
- Clear proof that the money was legitimate for you
- Formal letters sent to the right people (not just one call)
- Records that you actually followed up

Most people have the papers but they're scattered. Calls get ignored. Complaints get "closed" but the freeze stays.

Unhold helps you turn what you have into something the bank can take seriously:
- You tell your story in your own words (guided so nothing important is missed)
- Upload documents — it verifies them and tells you right away if something looks off
- Gets everything into one sealed package with proof of when and what you uploaded
- Gives proper letter formats (L1, L2, L3) that match what SBI usually expects
- Keeps a full record of every step you took

You remain in full control. You decide what to send and when.

**What you get right now**
- Guided questions (no login needed)
- Upload with live checking
- Timeline of what the system did
- Next actions to take
- Letter drafts you can copy and send

**Important truth**
This is not magic. It won't unfreeze your account by itself. It won't send anything for you. Some cases still take time and may need the police or Ombudsman.

But for many stuck innocent people, having a clean, verified, timestamped package + proper letters is what finally moves things.

**Direct answer to your question: "Why not make the product send the mails + attach PDFs/photos automatically so user just checks responses?"**

We will **never** add that.

Reasons (non-negotiable):
- Project rules forbid auto-send completely. Letters are "copy-only".
- If the app auto-sends on your behalf and something goes wrong, *you* can get into legal trouble with the bank or cyber cell. They want to know *you* personally sent it.
- The proof system only works if *you* are the one who actually sends and then marks proof.

What Unhold does instead:
- Gives you the ready letter text.
- Gives you the sealed bundle PDF (all your documents inside).
- Tells you the correct order and to whom (L1 to branch first, etc.).
- Makes attaching and sending easy.

You copy the text, attach the PDF yourself, send from your own email, then tell Unhold "I sent it" by uploading proof.

That's the line we will never cross.
