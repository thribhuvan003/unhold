**Body:**
If your account is restricted after a cyber complaint / UPI chain (or you’re not sure who ordered it), the hard part is often: *what papers, what to ask the bank in writing, and who can actually change the hold*.

I built **Unhold** (free, no account):

1. Short questions (bank, freeze type, who you think ordered it)
2. Checklist of papers for that situation
3. A **draft** branch letter from your answers — you review blanks, copy/print, send yourself
4. Suggested next steps (bank details first for many cyber holds; different asks for court/tax)

**What it is not:** not RBI, not police, not a lawyer, not an “unfreeze agent”. It never contacts the bank. Outcomes are always the bank/authority.

## Sticky FAQ replies

**Q: Will this unfreeze my account?**  
A: No tool can promise that. Banks/authorities decide. Unhold only helps you organise and draft.

**Q: Do you send the letter?**  
A: No. You copy/email/print and send. Always.

**Q: Is this a law firm?**  
A: No. Not legal advice. Independent lawyer if you need one.

**Q: Why upload documents?**  
A: Optional per checklist, private storage, to ground the draft and proof pack. Never upload OTP/PIN/full Aadhaar.
